[b]Welcome to Anoniem Chat[/b]

This is a high-security, privacy-focused messaging platform.
All messages are [b]end-to-end encrypted[/b] in your browser.

[b]Commands:[/b]
/nick <name>  - Change your display name
/me <action>  - Perform an action
/who          - See who is in the room
/id           - Show your info and room hash
/motd         - Show message of the day
/help         - Show this help text
/clear        - Clear your local chat history
/exit         - Leave the chat

[b]Privacy Note:[/b]
Nothing is stored on the server. Refreshing or exiting wipes everything.
To talk with others, share your [b]Room Key[/b] securely.
